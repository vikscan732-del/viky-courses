Refund Policy

Please read our refund policy carefully before purchasing any course.

Digital Products

All courses sold on course49 are digital products. Once access has been provided, refunds are generally not available.

Payment Issues

If your payment is successful but you do not receive access to your purchased course, please contact our support team. We will verify your purchase and resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Duplicate Payments

If you are charged more than once for the same course due to a technical error, please contact us with your payment details. After verification, any approved refund will be processed.

Refund Processing

If a refund request is approved after verification, the refund will be processed through the original payment method. Processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

Contact Support

If you have any questions regarding payments or refunds, please contact us through our official Telegram support bot. Please include your Payment ID and the email address used for the purchase to help us verify your request.
